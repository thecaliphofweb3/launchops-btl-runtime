FAQ

Questions, answered.

Is it free?

Yes. Free to enter, open to anyone anywhere. No fees or tiers.

Do I have to use the runtime?

Yes. Every project must call the BTL runtime. Registrants receive a key and free credits.

Do I need a team?

No. Solo builders are welcome. Teams can include up to four people.

Who owns the IP?

You do. BTL makes no claim on anything you build.

How are prizes paid?

In USD by bank transfer, PayPal, or stablecoin, depending on what works for your country.
