# Judging Rubric

Score each project out of 100.

## Runtime use: 30

- The project calls the BTL runtime in the core user flow.
- The team can explain why the gateway matters.
- The implementation uses relevant runtime capabilities such as model routing, streaming, tools, retrieval, memory, or embeddings.

## Product value: 25

- The user problem is clear.
- The product gives a useful result quickly.
- The demo shows a believable user workflow.

## Execution: 20

- The product works during judging.
- The setup path is understandable.
- The team handled failure states or scope limits honestly.

## Originality: 15

- The idea is differentiated.
- The team shows domain insight, taste, or a clever technical pattern.

## Demo quality: 10

- The demo is concise.
- The runtime call is easy to verify.
- The next step is obvious.
